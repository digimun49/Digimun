Privacy Policy

At DigiMax, your privacy is important to us. We collect only the essential information — such as your email address and name — to provide you with secure access to our platform, deliver notifications, and maintain your account.

Automatic Data Collection

When you access or use our platform, certain technical information is collected automatically to maintain the security and integrity of our services. This includes your IP address, device type and fingerprint, browser and operating system information, screen resolution, timezone, approximate geographic location (country and city derived from your IP address), and language preferences.

This data is collected as a standard industry security practice and is used exclusively for account protection, fraud prevention, abuse detection, and service optimization. IP-based geolocation is processed through trusted third-party services solely for security purposes — no other personal information is shared with these providers.

DigiMax does not offer, promote, or endorse any form of investment, trading, or financial advice. All information, tools, and data provided are strictly for educational and market analysis purposes only, and should not be interpreted as a recommendation to buy, sell, or invest in any financial instrument.

No Refund Policy

All payments made to DigiMax are final and non-refundable. We do not issue refunds under any circumstances — including, but not limited to, the non-receipt of confirmation or login emails caused by technical issues such as spam/junk filtering, incorrect email entry, or temporary server delays.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services — such as EmailOctopus — for email communications and account-related notifications. Your email address may be securely stored with these providers exclusively for operational contact purposes. DigiMax does not sell, rent, or misuse your personal data for any unrelated activities.
