Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use Digimun Pro services.

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Digimun Pro mobile application and website (digimun.pro).

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Email Address: For account creation, login, and communication
  • Username / User ID: For account identification and verification
  • Login Credentials: Stored securely for authentication purposes
  • App Usage Data: Basic, non-sensitive data to improve user experience
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our support channels

We do NOT collect sensitive financial information such as bank details, card numbers, or government ID numbers.

2 Why We Collect Your Data

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation & Login: To create and manage your user account
  • User Verification: To verify your identity and access permissions
  • Premium/Bot Access Approval: To manage subscription and service access
  • App Functionality: To ensure the app works correctly for you
  • Security: To protect your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

3 Data Storage & Security

We take your data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • All user data is transmitted securely using encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • We use Firebase for secure authentication and data storage
  • Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits are performed to maintain data protection

4 Data Sharing

Your privacy is important to us:

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data may be processed by trusted services (e.g., Firebase) strictly for app functionality and security purposes. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection standards.

5 Third-Party Advertisements

Digimun Pro does not display third-party advertisements. Your experience is ad-free.

If this policy changes in the future, we will update this document accordingly.

6 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: support@digimun.pro

7 Account & Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Upon request, we permanently delete the user account and associated data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe (typically 7-14 business days).

To request account deletion, email us at support@digimun.pro with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

8 Age Restriction

Digimun Pro is intended for users aged 18 years and above.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18 years of age. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such data from our servers.

9 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance.

Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
