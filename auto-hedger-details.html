❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my license key? ▼
Usually within 5-15 minutes after payment verification. Your key (format: DG-XXXXXXXX) will be sent via Telegram.
Can I use it on multiple trading platforms? ▼
Yes! Auto Hedger works on ANY trading website — Quotex, IQ Option, Pocket Option, Binance, Exnova, or any other broker. All with the same license key.
How does the device limit work? ▼
Each plan has a device limit (1-5). Your browser's unique fingerprint is recorded on first activation. Each different browser counts as a separate device. Need to switch? Contact us on Telegram (@digimun49) for an instant device reset.
What happens when my license expires? ▼
The widget will show a "License Expired" message. Simply purchase a new license to continue using Auto Hedger.
Is it safe to paste code in my browser? ▼
Yes — the code loads our official widget from digimun.replit.app. It only creates a trading overlay on your screen. It does not access your broker credentials or personal data.
Why doesn't the bookmark method work on Safari? ▼
Safari blocks javascript: bookmarklets for security. Use the Safari Address Bar method or iOS Shortcuts method instead — both work perfectly. See the "iPhone / iPad" tab in the setup guide above.
Does Auto Hedger guarantee profits? ▼
No. Auto Hedger is a trade execution tool — it automates clicking and timing. Results depend on market conditions and your strategy. Always use proper risk management.
