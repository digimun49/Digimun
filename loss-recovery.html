💡 Risk Management Tips

  • 1 Never invest more than you can afford to lose. Set a strict daily loss limit.
  • 2 Use proper position sizing - risk only 1-2% of your capital per trade.
  • 3 Don't chase losses by increasing trade sizes after a losing streak.
  • 4 Take breaks after losses to avoid emotional trading decisions.
  • 5 Follow signals consistently and avoid overtrading during volatile markets.
