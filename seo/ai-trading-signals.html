How AI Generates Trading Signals

Overview

AI signal systems follow a multi-step process: data collection (monitoring price action, candlesticks, volume, and momentum across multiple timeframes), pattern recognition (using machine learning to identify chart patterns like support/resistance, trend lines, and candlestick formations), confidence scoring (assigning a probability score based on how many confirming factors align), and signal output (delivering direction, confidence level, reasoning, and entry timing).

Understanding Confidence Scores

Overview

Most AI signal systems assign confidence scores to each signal. A score of 51-65% indicates a marginal edge — consider skipping. 66-80% represents a solid trade setup worth taking.

Key Details

81-95% means multiple strong factors are aligned — these are your best opportunities. Never trade signals below 60% confidence, and increase your conviction when scores exceed 80%.
