Ichimoku Cloud Strategy for Binary Options

The Ichimoku Cloud (Ichimoku Kinko Hyo) is a comprehensive indicator that defines support and resistance, identifies trend direction, gauges momentum, and provides trading signals all in one view.

How Ichimoku Works

The cloud is formed by the Senkou Span A and B lines. Price above the cloud is bullish, below is bearish. The Tenkan-sen and Kijun-sen crossovers provide entry signals. The Chikou Span confirms momentum.

Recommended Settings

Tenkan: 9, Kijun: 26, Senkou B: 52

Best Use Case

Complete trend analysis on 5-minute and above charts. Ichimoku is particularly effective when combined with other indicators and price action analysis for confirmation.

Ichimoku Trading Strategy

Enter CALL when price is above the cloud and Tenkan crosses above Kijun. Enter PUT when price is below the cloud and Tenkan crosses below Kijun. Cloud color change signals major trend shifts.

Tips for Using Ichimoku in Binary Options

  • Combine with trend analysis — Always confirm Ichimoku signals with the overall market trend
  • Use multiple timeframes — Check the signal on both 1-minute and 5-minute charts
  • Wait for confirmation — Don't enter on the first signal; wait for candlestick confirmation
  • Practice on demo first — Test your Ichimoku strategy on a demo account before trading real money
  • Apply money management — Never risk more than 1-3% of your account per trade
