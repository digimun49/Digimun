Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my signals?

After your payment is approved, log in to your account and navigate to the Signal Dashboard. If you don't see access, please submit a ticket above.

How long does payment approval take?

Payment approval typically takes 1-24 hours. If it's been longer, please contact us with your payment details.

Can I change my registered email?

Yes, submit a ticket with your current and new email addresses, and we'll update it for you.

What platforms are supported?

DigiMax signals work with Quotex, IQ Option, Exnova, Pocket Option, and Binomo.
