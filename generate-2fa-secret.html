Setup Instructions:

  1. Open Google Authenticator app on your phone
  2. Tap + and select "Scan QR code" or "Enter setup key"
  3. Scan the QR code above OR enter the secret key manually
  4. Go to Netlify Dashboard → Site settings → Environment variables
  5. Add new variable: ADMIN_TOTP_SECRET = [your secret key]
  6. Redeploy your site
  7. DELETE this page after setup!
