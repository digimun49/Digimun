4 Digimun 1-Minute Trading Concept (Safety Margin Logic)

Core Settings

  • Timeframe: 1 Minute
  • Expiry: 1 Minute
  • Entry Window: First 30 Seconds only

🟢 UP Signal

  • Wait for pullback
  • Candle must form a lower wick
  • Price should move down first
  • Entry must be within 30 seconds
  • If conditions fail = Skip trade

🔴 DOWN Signal

  • Wait for pullback
  • Candle must form an upper wick
  • Price should move up first
  • Entry must be within 30 seconds
  • If conditions fail = Skip trade

Trade expires with candle close.
