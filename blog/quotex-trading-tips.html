Quotex Trading Tips: 10 Proven Ways to Win More Trades

March 15, 20267 min readBy Digimun Pro Team

Quotex has become one of the most popular binary options platforms globally. But popularity doesn't mean easy profits. Here are 10 proven tips that experienced Quotex traders use to maintain a high win rate and grow their accounts consistently.

1. Master the Demo Account First

Quotex offers a free demo account with $10,000 virtual money. Use it. Spend at least 2 weeks practicing your strategy on demo before risking real money. If you can't be profitable on demo, you won't be profitable on a live account.

2. Trade During Peak Hours

The best trading hours on Quotex are during the London-New York session overlap (13:00-17:00 GMT). This is when Forex pairs have the most liquidity and the clearest trends. Avoid trading during the Asian session unless you're specifically trading JPY pairs.

3. Focus on 2-3 Currency Pairs

Don't scatter your attention across 20 different assets. Pick 2-3 pairs and learn their behavior deeply. Recommended pairs for beginners: EUR/USD, GBP/USD, and USD/JPY. Each pair has its own personality — learn it.

4. Use the 1-Minute Chart with 5-Minute Confirmation

Check the 5-minute chart for the trend direction. Then switch to the 1-minute chart to find precise entry points. This dual-timeframe approach filters out a lot of noise and increases accuracy.

💡
Pro Tip: On Quotex, you can open multiple chart tabs. Keep your 5-minute trend chart open alongside your 1-minute entry chart for quick reference.

5. Never Trade Against Strong Momentum

If you see 5+ consecutive green candles, don't place a PUT thinking "it has to reverse." Strong momentum often continues longer than you expect. Wait for the momentum to exhaust and show signs of reversal before trading against it.

6. Avoid the First 5 Minutes After Market Open

The first 5 minutes of any trading session are chaotic. Spreads are wide, price action is erratic, and signals are unreliable. Wait for the market to settle before placing your first trade.

7. Use Proper Trade Sizing

On Quotex, you can trade with as little as $1. Use this to your advantage:

  • Account under $50: Trade with $1 per trade
  • Account $50-$200: Trade with $1-3 per trade
  • Account $200-$1000: Trade with 1-2% of your balance
  • Account above $1000: Stick to 1% per trade strictly

8. Watch for Key Support and Resistance Levels

The most reliable trades happen at well-defined support and resistance levels. Mark these levels on your chart and wait for price to reach them before entering. A CALL at strong support or a PUT at strong resistance has a significantly higher win rate than random entries.

9. Keep a Trading Journal

After every trading session, record:

  • Which pairs you traded
  • Your entry reasons
  • Win/loss result
  • What you could have done better

After 2 weeks of journaling, you'll start seeing patterns in your own behavior that you can improve.

10. Use AI Signals as a Second Opinion

Even experienced traders benefit from a second opinion. AI signal tools like Digimun Pro can analyze charts and confirm (or reject) your trade idea. When your analysis and the AI agree, you have a much higher probability trade.
