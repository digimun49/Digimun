📋 Step-by-Step Guide

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free Digimun Pro account using your email address. This will be your registered email for all communications and access approvals.

Important:
  • Use a valid email you check regularly
  • This email will receive your access approval
  • Remember your login credentials
2

Choose Your Bot

After logging in, go to your dashboard and browse our available bots. Each bot offers different features and pricing options. Choose the one that best fits your trading needs.

Available Options:
  • Digimun Pro - Full signal access for all markets
  • Digimaxx - Enhanced signals with analysis
  • DigimunX AI - AI-powered chart scanner
  • Day Pass, 3-Day, 7-Day, or Lifetime access
3

Make Payment

Complete your payment using one of our supported methods. We accept Binance Pay (crypto) and local currency payments via direct transfer.

Payment Methods:
  • Binance Pay (USDT, BTC, ETH, BNB)
  • Local currency via bank transfer
  • Contact support for other payment options
4

Send Payment Proof

After making payment, take a clear screenshot of your payment confirmation. Send this screenshot to our admin team via Telegram or Support Ticket along with your registered email address.

Include in Your Message:
  • Payment screenshot (clear and complete)
  • Your registered email address
  • Which bot/plan you purchased
  • Transaction ID (if available)
5

Get Approved

Our admin team will verify your payment and approve your account. Once approved, you'll receive confirmation and can immediately access your purchased bot.

What Happens Next:
  • Admin reviews your payment (usually within 1-2 hours)
  • Your account gets activated for the purchased bot
  • You can start using signals immediately after approval
  • Access duration depends on your chosen plan
6

Start Trading

Log in to your dashboard, select your approved bot, and start receiving trading signals. Use the signals responsibly and always follow proper risk management.
